Free users can convert files up to 100MB in size. Premium users can convert files up to 2GB with our enterprise plan supporting even larger files.

No, you can use our basic conversion tools without creating an account. However, creating an account gives you access to conversion history, saved files, and additional features.

Most conversions are completed within seconds or minutes, depending on the file size and complexity. Large video files may take longer to process.
